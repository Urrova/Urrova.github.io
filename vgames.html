Arcade Invasion

This is a shoot em up where you go trought the worlds of some classic videogames, trying to stop POLBIUS 2.0, an arcade machine with the ability of controlling other machines.

Story:

A new machine arrived at an arcade center, called ... POLYBIUS v2.0. It invaded the other machines so that people hated video games, and plans to spread to the entire planet. But there is a kind of salvation. The player (you), who will drive to a ship to free the classic videogames of that arcade center, and prevent the polybius from expanding! Are you ready to play, player?

This game is arcade format. You can save the game but when your life is over, the game will be deleted, and you will have a new score on the high score table.
